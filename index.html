Services

What we do

Modular payload systems deployed from survey drones. One platform, multiple mission profiles.

◉

Below-Canopy 3D Capture

Imaging payload lowered through canopy gaps. 3D models of forest understory without ground access.

◓

Precision Treatment

Targeted herbicide application in dense forest. Operator-guided payload with real-time visual feedback. Reaches what aerial spray can't.

◎

Remote Sensor Relay

Automated data retrieval from ground sensor networks. Payload descends, collects, retracts. No ground crew required.

Approach

The tethered advantage

▱ Drone — full GPS above canopy
Payload — below canopy, on task

Drone stays above. Payload goes below.

Full GPS lock and obstacle avoidance above canopy. Payload descends through natural gaps to the work zone. No autonomous under-canopy flight, no ground crew.
